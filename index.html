About Me

I am currently a masters student of library and information science at Simmons University in Boston, Massachusetts. I am in the archives management concentration. I grew up in Shrewsbury, Massachusetts, just outside of Worcester, and am currently living in Brookline, Massachusetts. I did my undergraduate degree at Fordham University Rose Hill, in the Bronx, New York.

My professional interests include archives, cataloging, and metadata creation. I am particularly interested in the role of libraries, archives, and museums in rerpesenting marginalized and traditionally underrepresented communities. I also have an interest in digital stewardship and digitization initiatives in libraries, archives, and museums. I have experience working in government archives as well.
